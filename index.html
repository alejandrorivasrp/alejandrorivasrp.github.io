My RWD Final Project

Life in Sacco is a testament to resilience and community spirit.

The people of Sacco, deeply attuned to the changing seasons, navigate their rhythms with grace and determination. In the face of challenges, they not only endure but also find unexpected beauty, thriving through collective strength and shared traditions.

This small community, nestled within the Cilento and Vallo di Diano National Park in the province of Salerno, belonging to the Campania region in Italy, embodies the timeless essence of rural life.
